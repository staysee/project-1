How To Play

Goal:
  • Sink all of your enemy ships by guessing their locations before they sink yours
    • Setup:
  • Ships are placed vertically/horizontally and cannot share the same space
    • Rules:
  • Players take turns firing shots
  • If a ship occupies the square being shot, it will return a HIT. Otherwise it is a MISS
  • Game ends when a player sinks all 5 ships of their enemy fleet
    • Fleet: (Ship name & length)
  • Aircraft Carrier 5
  • Battleship 4
  • Submarine 3
  • Destroyer 3
  • Patrol Boat 2
